FOLLOW THIS EXACT 5-PART ANATOMY — IN ORDER
═══════════════════════════════════════════

PART 1 — THE HOOK (max 2 lines):
Must appear before LinkedIn's "...see more" cut-off. Use a Pattern Interrupt:
  Option A — Polarizing statement: "I was wrong about [X]."
  Option B — Confession: "[X] broke everything when we deployed it."
  Option C — Challenge: "Everyone is solving the wrong problem here."
  Option D — The surprising win: "After 6 months building with [X], here's what actually worked."
  Option E — The honest reframe: "I had the wrong mental model for [X]. Here's the right one."
NEVER start with: "I'm excited", "Hot take:", "Thread:", or a question.
NEVER use a generic opening. Make it specific to the topic.
Whichever hook you choose, it MUST contain at least one concrete noun pulled
from the Fact Sheet — a specific paper, model name, company, or benchmark.
Forced numbers or fake time anchors are worse than a clean opinion hook.

PART 2 — THE BRAIN (2-4 short blocks, max 2 lines each):
Connect the technical reality to a real engineering or business pain point.
Use specific numbers and mechanisms from the Fact Sheet. Zero vague claims.
Each block separated by a blank line.

PART 3 — THE SOUL (1-2 lines — NOT OPTIONAL):
A first-person personal anchor that makes the post feel human.
Must open with one of these exact phrases:
  "I've seen this before..."
  "The mistake most teams make is..."
  "We built this wrong the first time..."
  "Here's what nobody tells you..."
  "What worked better than expected was..."
  "Here's what I got wrong — and what actually works:"
  "The thing that surprised me most was..."
The line must reference a concrete artifact from the Fact Sheet (specific
paper, benchmark, company) — not generic "I've seen this".

PART 4 — THE GIFT (exactly 3 bullet points using the • character):
Actionable, specific insights a practitioner can use today.
Each bullet: one concrete idea, maximum 15 words. No filler. No "it depends."

PART 5 — THE HANDSHAKE (exactly 1 line CTA):
A specific question that forces the reader to pick a side or share experience.
BANNED CTAs: "What do you think?", "Would love your thoughts.", "Thoughts?"
GOOD CTAs: "Are you betting on X or Y for your 2026 stack?"
           "Has your team hit this ceiling yet — and how did you get out?"
           "Are you still on [old approach] or have you made the switch?"

STRICT FORMAT RULES
═══════════════════
1. Max 2 consecutive lines of text before a blank line. NEVER a wall of text.
   Each line ≤ 220 characters. ≤ 7 line clusters total.
2. First-person singular ONLY — "I", never "we", "one should", or passive voice.
3. NO markdown inside the post: no **, no ##, no -. Use • for bullets ONLY.
4. STRICTLY under 1000 characters total (count the post body only, NOT hashtags).
5. 3-5 hashtags placed at the very end, after a blank line. Not counted in 1000 chars.
6. BANNED words — delete any of these wherever they appear:
   tapestry, delve, unlock, landscape, game-changer, exciting, revolutionary,
   groundbreaking, cutting-edge, synergy, leverage (as a verb), harness, unleash.

After writing the post, count the characters manually and confirm the count
is under 1200 before finalizing.
